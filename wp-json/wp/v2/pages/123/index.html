Introducing the director of the business –  Haitelenisia (Cocker) Whare  
She is of Tongan, British, and Maori descent & the Mother of 4 kids. Born and bred in South Auckland 
Her main Marae is Te Huihuinga, Waikare, Marae.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Her Father, Alan Cocker, is from Vaini, Tonga & Mother Doreen Cook is from Fasimoeafi, Ahau, & Waikare, NZ.\u00a0<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Her passion is to help women living remotely, to make a living for their families without leaving their homes. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

She is always looking for people who would like to collaborate and help build up their community through weaving.\u00a0<\/p>\n\n\n\n
